Autonomous Line-Following Robot
Hardware / Nov 2025
Designed and built an Arduino-based line-following robot capable of autonomous navigation. Implemented a PID control algorithm with potentiometers for real-time tuning of PID gains, and soldered and integrated photoresistors, LEDs, and motors for line detection and path correction. Designed and 3D-printed the chassis in Onshape to house all components. Placed 2nd overall across a set of evaluation events, including a drag race, frequency sweep, and loop course.
